Flowers Name

Rose
Rose flowers are a popular crop for both domestic and commercial cut flowers. There are over three hundred species rose native to Asia, used for Perfume, Food, drink and Medicine with very rich in vitamin C.
Gerbera
Gerbera flowers are very popular cut flowers and also an important commercially flower used as a model organism. Gerbera is from the daisy family or Asteraceae and important ornamental crops for the cut flower industry with medicinal properties.
Tulip
Tulip flowers are grown only in Srinagar, which is also the largest tulip garden in Asia. The garden consisting of seven terraces and situated on the foothills of Zabarwan Range, grown along with hyacinths, daffodils and ranunculus.
Carnation
Carnation flowers or clove pink are used for medical purposes, fragrance used for vinegar, beer and wine. Dianthus caryophyllus are one of the fifth most used cut flower in the world along with chrysanthemum and tulip.
Chrysanthemum
Chrysanthemum is best for horticultural purposes and Chrysanthemum Ã— morifolium is the most important hybrid from Indian chrysanthemum.
Orchid
Orchid family flowers are the most expensive flowers in the world and few of them are endemic orchids of India, native to the Western Ghats, states of Assam and Meghalaya.
Lilium
True lilies or Lilium group of flowering plants are widely grown in the garden as ornamental and planted as bulbs in the dormant season. Mostly hybrids lilies are grown in the garden like Asiatic hybrids, Trumpet lilies and Oriental hybrids
